Privacy Policy — AgroChain

Last updated: 7 June 2026

AgroChain (“the App”, “we”, “us”) provides farm-to-consumer traceability for wheat and sugarcane supply chains in Pakistan using a permissioned blockchain (Hyperledger Fabric). This policy explains what data we collect, why, and your rights. By using AgroChain you agree to this policy.

Contact: saqibutm@outlook.com
Data controller: AgroChain Research Project — NRPU, Pakistan

1. Who this applies to

  • Supply-chain participants (farmers, collection/procurement centers, transporters, warehouses, mills, distributors, retailers, laboratories, regulators) who sign in and record transactions.
  • Consumers who scan a product QR code to verify it. Consumers can use core verification features without creating an account.

2. Data we collect

a. Information you provide

  • Account / entity identifiers — username and entity/farmer/mill IDs you enter.
  • Supply-chain records — batch IDs, crop variety, quantity, dates, custody transfers, and (for laboratories) quality results such as moisture, protein, gluten, and contamination indicators.
  • Consumer issue reports — if you report a problem: product ID, optional district, and your description.

b. Information collected automatically

  • Location (precise and/or approximate) — captured only at the moment you record a harvest or custody transfer, to geotag where that event occurred. The App does not track your location continuously or in the background.
  • App activity — the supply-chain actions you perform, needed to maintain the ledger.

c. Camera

The camera is used only to scan product QR codes in real time. Camera images/frames are not collected, stored, or transmitted.

We do not collect contacts, messages, browsing history, biometrics, or advertising identifiers, and the App contains no third-party advertising.

3. How we use your data

  • To record and verify supply-chain events on the blockchain ledger.
  • To display product traceability journeys, quality reports, and GPS routes.
  • To compute dashboard metrics and fraud/anomaly alerts (e.g. weight variance, quality failures).
  • To authenticate supply-chain participants and authorize permitted actions.

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Blockchain (important)

AgroChain writes supply-chain records to a permissioned blockchain ledger. By design, ledger entries are immutable and cannot be edited or deleted once committed. Please avoid entering unnecessary personal information into supply-chain records. Identifiers stored on the ledger are limited to what is required for traceability.

Access to the ledger is restricted to authorized organizations on the network (e.g. farmers, mills, regulators, laboratories) under their respective permissions.

5. Offline storage and sync

To support areas with poor connectivity, AgroChain stores your pending entries and language preference locally on your device (encrypted app storage) and automatically syncs them to the network when a connection is available. Clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the App removes this local copy.

6. Data sharing

We share data only:

  • With authorized organizations participating in the AgroChain network, as required for traceability.
  • With service providers that host the backend/network infrastructure on our behalf, under confidentiality obligations.
  • Where required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS to our servers and TLS between blockchain nodes).

7. Data retention

  • Off-chain account and operational data is retained while your account is active or as needed to provide the service, then deleted or anonymized.
  • On-chain records are permanent and immutable by the nature of blockchain (see §4).

8. Your rights and choices

  • Permissions: grant or revoke Camera and Location permissions any time in your device settings. Revoking them disables the related features (QR scanning, geotagging) but you can still use the rest of the App.
  • Access / correction / deletion: request access to, correction of, or deletion of your off-chain personal data by contacting us. On-chain records cannot be deleted; where feasible we will dissociate off-chain identifiers.
  • Account closure: contact us to deactivate your account.

9. Children’s privacy

AgroChain is intended for agricultural and commercial users and the general public. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date and, where appropriate, an in-app notice.
